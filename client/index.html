Frequently asked questions

Helpful answers for families

Contact the school directly when you need the latest class availability, fees, admission documents or boarding information.

Which classes are open for 2026/2027?

Admissions enquiries are accepted for JSS 1 through SSS 3. Final placement and availability are confirmed by the school.

Can I apply fully online?

No. The admission process is physical. Use the online enquiry form to request information, then collect and submit the official form at the school.

Does the school offer boarding?

Yes. Sherman Nagel Adventist High School offers both day and boarding programmes.

Is the school only for Adventist students?

The school is grounded in Seventh-day Adventist values and provides a welcoming Christ-centred secondary education. Contact the school for any family-specific questions.

Where can I find the fees and school calendar?

Current documents will appear in the resources section when published. You can also call or email the school to request the latest information.
